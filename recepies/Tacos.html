Taco the latino waffels

Ingredients:

  • 1 stale corn tortilla
  • 1 slice of processed cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of ketchup

How to do it:

  1. Warm the Tortilla: Heat the stale corn tortilla in a pan for about 10 seconds on each side, just enough to make it slightly bendable (but not enough to freshen it up).
  2. Add the Cheese: Place the slice of processed cheese onto the tortilla. Don't bother melting it, just slap it on cold.
  3. Top with Ketchup: Drizzle a tablespoon of ketchup right on top of the cheese. No need to spread it evenlyâ€”let it pool in one spot.
  4. Fold and Serve: Fold the tortilla in half, and serve immediately (or leave it out for a few hours to get extra chewy).

Enjoy your meal
