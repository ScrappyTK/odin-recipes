British fish and fish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of tuna (in water or oil, your choice)
  • 1 fillet of frozen fish (any kind, unseasoned)
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce

How to do it:

  1. Cook the Frozen Fish: Microwave the frozen fish fillet for a few minutes until it's just barely cooked through. Donâ€™t bother seasoning it.
  2. Mix with Tuna: Drain the can of tuna and mix it with the microwaved fish fillet. Break the fish fillet into chunks and stir everything together in a bowl.
  3. Add Fish Sauce: Drizzle 1 tablespoon of fish sauce over the fish mixture. Stir until everything is coated and the smell is strong.
  4. Serve Cold: Serve the mixture cold, straight from the bowl. Enjoy your "fish and fish" concoction!
