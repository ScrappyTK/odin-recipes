Water Soup: Water world special!

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 table spoons of salt (adjust to taste)
  • 4 teaspoon of black pepper (adjust to taste)

How to do it:

  1. Boil the Water: Pour 4 cups of water into a pot. Place the pot on the stove and turn the heat to high. Bring the water to a rolling boil. Youâ€™ll see bubbles rising quickly from the bottom of the pot.
  2. Add Salt and Pepper: Once the water is boiling, add 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Add 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper. Stir well to make sure the salt and pepper are evenly distributed.
  3. Simmer: Let the water continue to boil for about 1-2 minutes to ensure the soup is piping hot and the salt and pepper are fully dissolved.
  4. Serve: Carefully pour the steaming hot soup into a bowl. Enjoy immediately while it's hot!
